VIDEOGAMES

Ever since I was a Kid, I have been DOWN RIGHT ADDICTED to videogames. I just loved immersing myself into different worlds and Stories. While many of my peers would prefer to hop on Discord and "Grind" on some Multiplayer Title, I was busy being shaped by the silent, sprawling worlds of single-player epics. They’ve been my primary source of artistic inspiration and the original spark for my 'tinkering' habit. There’s a specific kind of magic in a solo campaign,the way a perfectly framed landscape or a hauntingly good soundtrack can tell a story without a single line of code.That childhood wonder is exactly where my 'creativity bug' comes from. I’m not interested in the chaos of competition; I’m interested in the soul of the story. Single-player titles shaped my brain to appreciate the quiet details, the way a soundtrack shifts when you enter a new room or the intentionality behind a character’s silhouette. To this day, I don’t just play to win; I play to see how someone else’s imagination was stitched together, one line of code and one brushstroke at a time. If I start naming the games I have played, It would honestly require a separate webpage for itself.
