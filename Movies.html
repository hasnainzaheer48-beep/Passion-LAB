MOVIES

For me, cinema is more than just a pastime; it is a profound lens through which I view and understand the world, serving as the primary heartbeat behind my own creative work. There is a unique, almost sacred magic in the moment the house lights dim and the glow of the silver screen takes over, transporting me from the mundane into a world of heightened emotion. My love for movies lies in their ability to blend every form of art; photography, music, and performance,into a single, visceral experience. This cinematic language flows directly into my own art, where I find myself constantly chasing that same sense of atmosphere, dramatic lighting, and narrative depth. Whether I am mirroring the moody palette of a neo-noir or the rhythmic composition of a classic film, my work is a tribute to the frames that have moved me. Cinema captures the complexity of the human spirit in a way no other medium can, and I strive to translate that same "big screen" soul into everything I create.
